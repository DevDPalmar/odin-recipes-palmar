how to make waffles


comedera.com


description


Waffles need no introduction, but let me tell you that it is a preparation made with flour, milk or water, sugar, egg, lard or butter. The mixture that results from combining all these ingredients is thick or dense, which goes through a cooking process for a super crispy and fluffy dish.


Ingredients


  • 100 gr of regular wheat flour, not leavened.

  • 2 eggs

  • 50 gr of melted butter

  • 50 ml milk

  • 3 tablespoons sugar

  • 3 tablespoons sugar

  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

  • 1 tablespoon vanilla

steps to follow


  1. Let's start our recipe by mixing in a bowl the wheat flour, the pinch of salt, the sugar and the baking powder. Mix until they are well integrated.

  2. In another bowl, place the eggs and beat until you get a frothy mixture.

  3. Add to the bowl of eggs, melted butter, milk and the tablespoon of vanilla.

  4. With a spatula, mix gently so as not to break up the air in the eggs.

  5. Now, we are going to integrate the flour to the mixture progressively and beat at medium speed until everything is incorporated and you get a thick creamy texture.

  6. Once our mixture is ready, proceed to heat your waffle iron. If necessary, you can grease it a little.

  7. It is time to pour a little of the mixture into the waffle iron, making sure that it covers the entire surface, but does not spill over.

  8. Cover your waffle iron and let it cook. It usually takes 5 to 6 minutes, depending on your machine. You will know when they are golden brown and delicious.

  9. Repeat the procedure with all the mixture until you have your waffles. Enjoy them
